Homemade Lasagna

Description

This mouthwatering homemade lasagna, a classic Italian dish, is perfect for family dinners or special occasions. Layers of tender pasta sheets nestle between a rich meat sauce seasoned with herbs and spices and a generous helping of melted cheese. A combination of cottage cheese, Parmesan, and mozzarella creates a creamy, indulgent texture, while eggs bind it all together. Even the most discerning of palates will enjoy this hearty, comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • Diced onion
  • Can of tomato sauce
  • Can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped parsley
  • 1 clove of crushed garlic
  • Sugar (a dash)
  • Dried basil
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles (uncooked)
  • Cottage cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat: Brown the ground meat in a skillet. Add onion and cook until translucent. Stir in canned tomato products, half the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until al dente.
  3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, remaining parsley, remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: Layer ingredients according to the recipe, starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella.
  5. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in preheated oven for about 30 minutes. Remove foil and continue baking until top is golden brown.

Note: In an oven preheated to 375 degrees F, this homemade lasagna should be perfectly baked in about 50 minutes (30-40 minutes covered, 5-10 minutes uncovered).
